Kickback Program

Unique Concept in the Netherlands

FlexPlek IQ offers a revolutionary approach to coworking: become both a member and supporter of our workspaces and receive significant kickbacks on your membership.

How It Works

  • Base cost per month: €2,200
  • Revenue above this threshold is shared with members
  • The kickback percentage varies based on the total revenue
  • The percentage is calculated as: ((new total - base cost) / base cost) × 100
  • For example, with €3,300: ((3300 - 2200) / 2200) × 100 = 50%
  • Members receive 50% of the excess amount
  • Distribution is calculated monthly
